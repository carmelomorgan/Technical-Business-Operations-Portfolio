## Purpose

The Enterprise Risk Treatment Plan establishes the strategy Northwind Health Solutions will use to address cybersecurity risks identified during the Enterprise Cybersecurity Assessment.

This document defines the selected treatment option for each identified risk, identifies responsible stakeholders, establishes implementation priorities, and outlines the expected outcomes of each remediation effort.

The objective is to reduce organizational risk while aligning cybersecurity improvements with business objectives, operational requirements, and available resources.

-----

## Risk Treatment Strategy

Northwind Health Solutions uses four standard risk treatment strategies.

| Treatment Option | Description                                                                                                  |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Mitigate         | Implement administrative, technical, or operational controls to reduce the likelihood or impact of the risk. |
| Accept           | Formally acknowledge the risk because additional mitigation is not currently justified.                      |
| Transfer         | Shift responsibility for portions of the risk through contracts, insurance, or third-party agreements.       |
| Avoid            | Eliminate the activity creating the risk by changing business processes or technology.                       |

For this assessment, the majority of identified risks will be mitigated through improvements to governance, documentation, and security operations.

## Risk Treatment Details

### R-001 – Security Governance

Treatment Strategy

Mitigate

Objective

Establish a formal cybersecurity governance program that improves executive oversight, accountability, and strategic decision-making.

Planned Activities

* Develop a Cybersecurity Governance Charter.
* Define governance roles and responsibilities.
* Establish quarterly governance meetings.
* Develop executive cybersecurity reporting.
* Document governance review procedures.

Expected Outcome

Cybersecurity governance becomes a repeatable and measurable business process supported by executive leadership.

Related Portfolio Documents

* Security Governance Documentation
* Security Metrics Dashboard
* Executive Summary

### R-002 – Enterprise Risk Management

Treatment Strategy

Mitigate

Objective

Create a centralized enterprise cybersecurity risk management process.

Planned Activities

* Maintain a centralized Enterprise Risk Register.
* Assign risk owners.
* Define review frequency.
* Track mitigation activities.
* Document residual risk.

Expected Outcome

## Conclusion

The Enterprise Risk Treatment Plan provides Northwind Health Solutions with a structured approach for reducing cybersecurity risk through prioritized, measurable, and risk-based remediation activities.

Implementation of this plan will strengthen governance, improve operational consistency, enhance executive visibility into cybersecurity risk, and increase alignment with the NIST Cybersecurity Framework. The activities identified in this document establish the foundation for future policy development, remediation tracking, and continuous improvement across the organization's cybersecurity program.
